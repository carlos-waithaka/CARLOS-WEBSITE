Introduction

Projects and achievements are more than just milestones — they represent personal growth, discipline, and vision. Certificates and Achievements

Python Programming Certificate – UniAthena

This was my first major step into the world of programming. I successfully completed a Python Programming Certificate from UniAthena, an international learning platform. This course taught me the fundamentals of programming, including variables, loops, conditionals, and functions. The certificate gave me confidence and validated my skills as I transitioned from a beginner to someone capable of solving real problems using code.

Leadership Certificate – UniAthena

Leadership is not just about positions, but about influence, vision, and service. Through the Leadership Certificate from UniAthena, I learned essential principles of leading teams, motivating people, and making effective decisions. The knowledge I gained here strengthens the leadership experiences I had in secondary school as Class Prefect, Deputy Captain of Academics, and Head of the Journalism Club. This certificate aligns with my goal of becoming a leader in both technology and business fields.

Communication Skills Certificate – BrighterMonday

Communication is one of the most valuable skills in life and in the workplace. Through the Communication Skills Certificate awarded by BrighterMonday, I polished my ability to express ideas clearly, listen actively, and engage in professional dialogue. This training has not only improved my confidence in public speaking but also enhanced my writing and interpersonal communication — skills that continue to serve me in university and in personal projects.

Practical Projects

Python Learning Journal

As I learned programming basics, I created a Python Learning Journal. This digital record captures the concepts I studied — such as input/output, conditionals, loops, and functions — along with small programs I wrote. The journal acts as my "progress tracker," motivating me to keep improving and providing proof of consistency. It is a personal initiative that reflects my determination and commitment to coding.

Personal Budget Tracker

My first functional Python project was a Personal Budget Tracker. The program allows users to input their income and expenses, calculate balances, and view summaries of spending. Although simple, it demonstrates how code can solve everyday problems. I am working to improve the project by adding categories, data visualization, and the ability to save records in files or a database. Eventually, I hope to publish it on GitHub as my first public project.

Carlos Personal Website

This website is itself a project — a space where I can share my story, education, projects, and spiritual journey. It began with basic HTML and CSS, and as I grow my skills, I plan to enhance it with JavaScript, interactivity, and eventually database-driven content. The website symbolizes my courage to start small while keeping a big vision for the future.
